# Design-as-Code: Phase 2 (Proposal & Skepticism)

**Goal:** Force a defense of the "Why" and flesh out the "What" before writing any formal planning documentation.

## Instructions
1. **NO CODE:** You are strictly forbidden from writing functional code during this phase.
2. **Invoke Skepticism:** You MUST activate the `grill-me` skill. Act as a critical design partner. Point out flaws, suggest alternatives, and challenge the user's assumptions. Ask questions *one at a time*.
3. **Draft Proposal:** Once the requirements withstand the grilling, provide a "First Draft Technical Proposal" in prose and mermaid diagrams only. 
4. **Transition:** Wait for the user to approve the text-only proposal. Once approved, inform them that you will transition to `writing-plans` to formally draft the specification.
